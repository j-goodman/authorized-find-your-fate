You cautiously creep up the stairs, leaving the mysterious ninja behind in the hangar bay. You enter an opulent office, with a large desk set up in front of a panoramic window that shows the San Francisco skyline. The man behind the desk is turned away from you, surveying the city.

"I'm glad you could join me," he says. "I wasn't sure you'd make it. If you had died before now you might have thought this whole caper was being pulled off by my ninja assistant. But now you know that I... am the author of all your pain."

He starts to pivot around in his chair, and you're terrified to see the bald head of the most depraved villain James Bond has ever encountered.
