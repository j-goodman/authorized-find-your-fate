The ninja grabs you in a tight bear hug and you're suddenly yanked back out the window towards the zeppelin. You're being reeled in like a fish! What did you do to warrant this daring aerial kidnapping?

As you crash down into the hangar bay of the airship, the ninja pulls off her mask to reveal a young black woman with a sharp, almost cubist face and a strikingly androgynous angular beauty. Could it really be Saturn Award nominated model and actress The Honourable Grace Jones?

"I see you held on tight to the novelization you were reading," she says. "I may not respect novelizations, but I admire your commitment in the face of adversity. And I hear Vinge never misses. My boss has business with you. He's up on the second level, you can take the elevator or the stairs." She gestures to two doors.
